# Font Options for Nexiler Website

## Best Google Fonts for Modern Tech/AI Websites:

### 1. **Inter** (Highly Recommended ⭐)
- **Style**: Clean, modern, highly readable
- **Best for**: Tech companies, SaaS platforms
- **Why**: Industry standard, excellent at all sizes, supports multiple weights
- **Import**: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap');`
- **CSS**: `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;`

### 2. **Outfit** (Recommended ⭐)
- **Style**: Geometric, modern, professional
- **Best for**: AI/Tech startups, innovative brands
- **Why**: Unique yet professional, great readability, modern feel
- **Import**: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Outfit:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap');`
- **CSS**: `font-family: 'Outfit', sans-serif;`

### 3. **Manrope**
- **Style**: Rounded, friendly, modern
- **Best for**: Approachable tech brands
- **Why**: Warm yet professional, excellent on screens
- **Import**: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Manrope:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap');`
- **CSS**: `font-family: 'Manrope', sans-serif;`

### 4. **Space Grotesk**
- **Style**: Bold, futuristic, distinctive
- **Best for**: Cutting-edge tech, AI companies
- **Why**: Unique character, strong brand presence
- **Import**: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Space+Grotesk:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap');`
- **CSS**: `font-family: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif;`

### 5. **Sora**
- **Style**: Elegant, modern, clean
- **Best for**: Premium tech services
- **Why**: Professional with personality, great hierarchy
- **Import**: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Sora:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap');`
- **CSS**: `font-family: 'Sora', sans-serif;`
